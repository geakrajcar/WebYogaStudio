INFO

We've prepared a little surprise for all of you on, on Tuesday, 26th of December, in our studio. :)
Since the space is limited, registrations are required (at the bottom of this page)!
We will start with a Vinyasa Flow Yoga Class **/*** led by Gea. Together we will flow through a fluid sequence of asanas, guided Pranayama and relaxation.
After the yoga class - it's time for Bhakti Yoga and chanting. Our devoted student Kristina Krajcar, who is studying Sanatana Dharma, will open the doors to the beautiful practice of chanting mantras.
We will close the evening with sweet vegan bites and healthy juice made by our student Martina Malic. You might have seen Martina practicing yoga in our studio, or in the local organic food store Zelena, where she is doing her magic daily and preparing all kinds of delicios food.
Did we mention that this event is open for everyone? And free? :) Registrations are required!
See you!
