CHAKRA VINYASA

In this three-days workshop we will experience 5 different practices for the first five chakras (from the 7-chakra system). Practices will include themed visualizations, movement meditations, Pranayama, mantras, mudras, themed Surya Namaskars and asanas which will all together awaken different qualities that we connect with each chakra, and help us to dive deeper into the more subtle parts of ourselves. Moving through the intelligently sequenced set of asanas we will flow towards a few advanced asanas to create space for stepping into the unknown and move our boundaries in a safe environment, learning a lot of modifications and assists along the way so every person can find their own expression of the asanas.

ABOUT GEA

Gea is a teacher of Integral Vinyasa Flow Yoga (RYT500, E-RYT200) and Prenatal Yoga (RPYT), all under the guidance of her dear teacher Snježana Nina Vukas in Sangha Om Yoga School (Zagreb, Croatia).
When she is not on a yoga mat or studying about yoga and anatomy, Gea loves to spend her time in nature, next to the sea or high in the mountains. She's passionate about traveling to new places, learning about their cultures and beauties of nature. Dogs. She is also passionate about dogs.
Beside the continuous teacher trainings, she always stays on the path, devoted to her personal daily practice and broadening the knowledge. She learned from and practiced with Shiva Rea, Simon Park, Tiffany Cruikshank, Maty Ezraty, Patrick Broome, Krishna Das, just to name a few.
